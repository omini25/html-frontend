Daabo provides device protection for individuals and businesses, helping with device security and device insurance. It serves thousands of businesses and individuals from Lagos, Nigeria with international ambition. It was founded by David Igiebor, Ajibola Adebayo and Sophia Abubakar in 2020 with a mission to making device insurance and security accessible to everyone. Daabo is currently a seed company.
